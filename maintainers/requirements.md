# Requirements to become a maintainer of BlissRom

Before you apply for maintainership to add your device to our official devices list, read following requirements carefully.

**Any failure to comply with following instructions will be liable for instant rejection of application. there wont be second chance.**

1 - You must own the device you are applying for official status. blind builds are strictly banned in all forms.

2 - You must have good knowledge of Git and Gerrit in general.

3 - You must maintain the said device unofficially for atleast span of 1 month with constant/periodic updates as when releases are done. Make sure builds are stable for daily usage.  We will be observing the activity and support provided by you in respective releases. The context of stability may differ for different devices, so explain regarding any exceptions.

4 - You must have your device sources available publicly with proper authorship on every commit. Hidden sources or private sources in any form is not allowed. DO NOT attempt to show some sources for public and different sources to build BlissRom, if found they will be rejected instantly and will be barred from applying in future. Kindly do not attempt to bluff us.

5 - Once applied DO NOT spam Team Members with status of application that will be ground for rejection. Allow us to review your application. We will respond within a week max from the date you applied.
